My Shoe Laces

On my foot, over the shoe Today I tied my laces, and it went free again. Like holding your memories, with lots of "if" and "but" in my brain. Talking of you and love, My heart gets cornered again. When the sun rises and office should be attained, I walk through the park and my laces gets entertained. It's like you held my hand on stairs not to fall and me…. just to fall again. The moment you smile,your laces stuck in bushes, Body hit the ground and your smile is joined by pain. Once again you stand and decide the story will not remain the same, With efforts, Your Day goes well but by the night you know... it's not your game. Because when sun kisses the sea, you fall for it and heart takes over your brain. "Shishir", Everyday I smile and tie up my thoughts well with sun rising again, But by the night my laces are free... to make me fall, so the story remains the same. -By the way, my laces are rebellious by nature ;) Happy Readings.

Fall in Love, again

Sometimes I pray to fall …..in love again And often I fear of the same, What if you forget me And change the surname. Only God knows the depth of sea of love in my heart, like me on the surface of existence he is a floating dead man. Perhaps, I'm ready to be burned alive In your love, if one spares my heart On your command. Closer we get, deeper we fall Not bound to egdes or body's state, It's gravity ….keeps you down With fights as friction, drives straight. love is like entropy of universe, It’s character, grows randomly and seems really insane. If love hits me again, It should make fall you first Then meet me this time but only with your name. I want my life to crawl out through the window of love, To see her maester..like a dream With a Smile.... Holding your hand. -From the place with your nameplate, Which is mine but miss you more.
