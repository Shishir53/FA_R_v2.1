Poem

My Shoe Laces

On my foot, over the shoe
Today I tied my laces, and it went free again.
Like holding your memories,
with lots of "if" and "but" in my brain.
Talking of you and love,
My heart gets cornered again.
When the sun rises and office should be attained,
I walk through the park and my laces gets entertained.
It's like you held my hand on stairs not to fall and me….
just to fall again.
The moment you smile,
your laces stuck in bushes,
Body hit the ground
and your smile is joined by pain.
Once again you stand and decide the story will not remain the same,
With efforts, Your Day goes well but by the night
you know... it's not your game.
Because when sun kisses the sea,
you fall for it and heart takes over your brain.
"Shishir", Everyday I smile
and tie up my thoughts well with sun rising again,
But by the night my laces are free...
to make me fall, so the story remains the same.

My laces are rebellious by nature. ;)
Happy Readings.
— Farji Aadami
